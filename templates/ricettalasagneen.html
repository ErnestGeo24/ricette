Lasagne alla Bolognese

Preparation
In this recipe, which has now become one of the dishes that made the history of Italian cuisine together with tagliatelle with Bolognese sauce, you will find all the information on how to prepare lasagna.
01
To make For lasagne alla bolognese you must first of all cut the Mozzarella Santa Lucia into cubes. Then let it drain off the excess water.
02
Then, put plenty of water, extra virgin olive oil and salt in a large saucepan. At this point, bring the salted water to a boil. Once boiling, cook the sheets of fresh egg pasta for about a minute.
03
Drain them and put them to dry on a clean cloth. At the end of the operation, take a baking dish, oil it on the bottom and on the edges and pour a layer of béchamel, preferably flavored with a sprinkling of nutmeg. Then, continue with a layer of pasta, a ladle of béchamel and two ladles of ragù (mix the two compounds with the aid of the ladle).
04
Then, evenly distribute a layer of strips of Galbanino il Saporito and one of diced Mozzarella Santa Lucia. Make all the layers in this way and continue like this until all the ingredients are used up. The last layer of egg pasta must be covered with bechamel, mozzarella and plenty of Galbanino il Saporito cheese, so that a nice crunchy crust forms on the dish during cooking.
05
Conclude your lasagna alla bolognese by baking them in a preheated oven at 200° for about 30 minutes. Once cooked, remove the preparation from the oven and serve hot.

Pasta alla carbonara

What we suggest you make at home is the classic recipe based on bacon, pecorino cheese and egg yolk. The bacon will be saut√©ed in a large pan with the oil until it becomes transparent but not too crunchy; in a saucepan with plenty of salted water, cook the spaghetti al dente; whisk the grated cheese, egg yolks and pepper in a bowl with quick movements.
As soon as the pasta is cooked, it must be drained immediately, put in the pan, mixed quickly and poured into a bowl with the eggs. At this point, immediately after pouring the spaghetti, we will mix everything to form a creamy sauce.
01
To make pasta carbonara you must first of all take a bowl. Add the egg yolks, the pecorino cheese and a generous pinch of freshly ground pepper. Whisk the mixture well.
02
When the operation is complete, boil the spaghetti in slightly salted water until al dente. In the meantime, take a frying pan and lightly fry the Pancetta Galbapancetta previously cut into julienne strips in a little extra virgin olive oil.
03
When cooked, drain the spaghetti, retaining a little of the cooking water ( the pasta should not be too dry). At this point, pour them into the bacon pan, remove them from the heat and mix them together.
04
Finally, add the beaten eggs and stir everything together vigorously. Serve your pasta carbonara immediately.

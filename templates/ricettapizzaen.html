Pizza Margherita

Get in the kitchen right away and try the following Margherita pizza recipe:
01
To make the Margherita pizza, put the flour, sugar and baking powder in a bowl.
02
Mix the ingredients with a spoon, then add the tepid water a little at a time, adding the salt at the end; knead by hand for about 10 minutes until the dough is smooth and velvety. In case of lumps, don't worry: break them up with the help of a fork and keep mixing faster to mix the dough.
03
Let it rise until doubled in volume inside the bowl, covered with a damp cloth or food film. In this way the base of your pizza will be able to become high and soft and acquire volume, thanks to the leavening. Make sure it is well covered, so as not to risk the surface drying out.
04
When the dough has risen, divide it into two parts of equal size and spread it with your fingertips on two greased trays. oil, pressing with your fingers with a movement from the center to the edges. Garnish with the tomato. If desired, leave to rise for a further 40 minutes directly in the pan. At the end of the leavening, bake in a static oven at 200째-220째 for 15-20 minutes. Better to wait for the last phase of cooking to insert the mozzarella, in order to prevent it from drying out; you will thus obtain a perfect result.
05
